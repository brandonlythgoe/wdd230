Gilbert Temple

The Beautiful Gilbert temple is located in the heart of San Tan Village in Gilbert Arizona. It provides a place of worship to all latter day saints in the gilbert area, as well as a beautiful building for all of gilberts residents to see. All are welcome to come and tour the temple grounds, and during Christmas time it is one of gilberts biggest attractions as you can walk the grounds filled with christmas lights.

Best Golf Courses In the Valley

Gilbert is simply a heaven on earth for Golfers. There are dozens or prestine golf courses ranging from begginer level to incredibly challenging. Thanks to the terrific weather here in Gilbert, residents are able to Golf Year round!

Check out Gilberts Finest Restaurants

Here in Gilbert there is an incredible amount of options when it comes to fine dining. From Authentic Mexican to Zesty Italian, Gilbert truly has it all. We reccomend checking our Business directory to see some of the hottest local restaurants here in Gilbert.

Family fun

Gilbert is a fantastic place to go out and ejnoy time together as a family. Venues such as Top Gold, Main Event, and Fat Cats are just a few of Gilberts family attractions.
