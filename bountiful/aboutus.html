Where Bountiful Began

Bountiful Foods began right here in this little home just outside Carslbad. Trevor Smith and his wife Emma started to make delicious fresh fruit drinks out of their home garden for freidns and family. Soon they realized just how unique it was to serve fresh organic fruit! They then started to sell their fruit and fruit drinks locally and year after year they slowly grew into what you now know as Bountiful Foods!
