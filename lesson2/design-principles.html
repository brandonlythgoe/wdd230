Brandon Lythgoe

Repitition

ESPN Homepage

espn.com

I love the ESPN website. It is so easy to use and just makes a ton of sense. I chose this website because I think it is a great example of repitition. I love how they reapeatedly use a logo for each sport and in the drop down menu you see a very similiar pattern across each sport. This allows the user to easily identify what they are looking for and feel at ease while using the site. It also gives the user a sense of familiarity as soon as they open up the website as they have seen those same logos many times before.

Color

Dunkin Donuts Homepage

dunkindonuts.com

I think the dunkin donuts website is great example of color and contrast. The orange and pink really pop, especially on the clean white background. Those two colors also look great together and don't clash, yet are unqiue to dunkin donuts. This simple feature helps the user to identify with dunkin donuts and appeals to us.

Visual Alignment

Code Art Homepage

codeart.com

The Code Art website is an excellent website, and is hard to capture in just one screenshot. This site does a wonderful job of exemplifying visual alignment and balance. Everything is optimized perfectly, and they do a great job of featuring the information that they want you to see. They also do a great job of keeping images and text centered, which makes for a clean crisp look.
