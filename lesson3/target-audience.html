Target Audience

The target Audience for this site is simply anyone involved in the Gilbert Community. This could be local buisness owners, residents, and even tourists. These people will come to the site for a variety of reasons rannging from networking, event planning, and consumer information.

Persona 1: Joe Smith

User Motivation- Joe Smith Is a Local Buisness Owner who is looking to network with other buisness owners in the community. He wants to learn helpful tactics from his peers, but more than anything wants to make an impact in the community. He is hoping to brainstorm ways to give back to the community and collaborate with other buisnesses in the area.

Persona 2: Susan Wells

User Motivation- Susan Wells is a local resident that is looking to support local buisnesses. In the past, she has shopped more often at Nation Wide Chains out of habit but wants to start helping out smaller buisnesses. She isn't sure however where to go, so is interested in looking at the Gilbert Chamber of Commerce to get some ideas.
